New Clothes and Accessories

I can use pre-fab patterns or make my own. I will make clothes for all ages and sizes of humanity along with some pet designs too. [infants (newborn thru toddlers), children, teenagers, womens, mens, and pets.]

Alterations and Mending

I can do alterations to help tailor your clothing to fit you the way you want it to fit; from taking it in to a smaller size or letting it out to accommodate extra growth. I can hem. I can add patches and can incorporate embroidery for personal touches. I can turn old clothes into a new look.

Wall Hangings, Blankets, and Quilts

I can make doll quilts, receiving blankets, lap blankets and quilts, twin, or queen size quilts. I can make king size quilt tops and source out the quilting.
